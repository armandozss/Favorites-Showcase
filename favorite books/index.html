All time favorite books

These are my favorite books of all times

  • Don Quixote
  • The Metamorphosis
  • Azul

  • Don Quixote

    • The title character of the novel, Don Quixote is a gaunt, middle-aged gentleman who, having gone mad from reading too many books about chivalrous knights, determines to set off on a great adventure to win honor and glory in the name of his invented ladylove, Dulcinea.

  • The Metamorphosis

    • The Metamorphosis by Franz Kafka is about a man named Gregor Samsa who becomes an insect. Until this incident, Gregor has worked hard to support his parents and sister. After he can no longer provide for their financial comfort, Gregor discovers that his family can indeed work.
